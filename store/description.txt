Drift — Stay Present Without Lifting a Finger

Drift is a smart activity keeper that maintains your online presence, prevents your computer from sleeping, and helps you take real breaks without the anxiety of appearing idle.

🌊 THREE ACTIVITY MODES
• Simple — Minimal cursor movement, lightweight and discreet
• Zen — Smooth circular motion for a calm, steady presence
• Human — Realistic activity patterns with variable speed, micro-movements, and natural pauses

⏰ SMART SCHEDULING
• Set work hours (e.g., Mon–Fri 9am–6pm) for automatic activation
• Configurable lunch break — your status changes naturally
• Quick timers: activate for 30m, 1h, 2h, or 4h with one click
• Works around your schedule, not against it

🧘 WELLNESS BREAK REMINDERS
Because everyone deserves real breaks:
• 20-20-20 Rule — Eye rest reminders every 20 minutes
• Stretch reminders every 45–60 minutes
• Hydration check-ins
• Fully customizable — enable what you need

🔍 PRESENCE DETECTION
• Detects if Microsoft Teams or Slack is open in your browser
• Visual indicator showing which apps are active
• Stay informed about your digital presence at a glance

🆓 100% FREE & OPEN SOURCE
• All features included — no premium tiers, no paywalls, no ads
• Open source on GitHub — audit the code yourself
• Free forever. No accounts, no subscriptions, no catch.

✨ DESIGNED FOR PROFESSIONALS
• Clean, modern dark UI with indigo accents
• Keyboard shortcut (Alt+Shift+D) for instant toggle
• Toolbar badge: green (active), red (inactive), yellow (scheduled)
• Zero data collection — everything stays on your device
• Available in English, Spanish, and Portuguese

🔒 PRIVACY FIRST
Drift operates entirely locally. No accounts, no servers, no data collection. Your activity data never leaves your browser.

⚡ LIGHTWEIGHT
Minimal resource usage. No background page — uses modern Manifest V3 service worker architecture for efficiency.

Drift exists because everyone deserves autonomy over their breaks. We don't promote avoiding work — we promote working without the constant anxiety of surveillance that distracts and stresses.
